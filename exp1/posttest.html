Post Test

Q1. The characteristics of IGBT is a combination of which of the following two devices?

a. SCR and TRIAC

b. MOSFET and SCR

c. SCR and BJT

d. MOSFET and BJT

Q2. Which of the following statements about an Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor(IGBT) is not true?

a. The IGBT is developed by combining the characteristics of a BJT and a MOSFET

b. The on-state losses of an IGBT are lesser than a MOSFET

c. The IGBT is slower than a BJT

d. The IGBT contains a parasitic thyristor

Q3. Which of the following is a demerit of IGBT?

a. High peak current capability

b. Low turn off time

c. Ease of gate drive

d. High turn off time

Q4. what does the term "bias" mean?

a. a DC voltage is applied to control the operation of device

b. the ratio of majority carriers to minority carriers

c. the amount of current across a diode

d. None of these

a. Output current depends on output voltage

b. Input current depends on output current

c. Output current depends on input current

d. Output current depends on input voltage
